About

This page is a portfolio highlighting the work I've done in the Thinkful program (see: "Thanks" tab for details). It contains my favorite projects that I felt brought the most to the table in terms of educational value. Inspiration in each project varies, all of which I won't bother getting in to. What I will get in to, however, is the inspiration behind this page.


If you didn't catch on initially, it's based off the Mortal Kombat character selection screen. The music being from that as well, I didn't make it, don't own it, etc. You get the picture. I did this because I firmly believe in having fun with what you're doing. Considering all of my projects having some weird element in them, I feel it works very well. There were a lot of moments where I felt like I wasn't going to be able to finish something, or just got frustrated with the project entirely. Particularly with this page, I had no idea what to do with my layout. There's a lot of very popular styles out there, but none of which I really felt would be good for a portfolio project. So, I wanted to do something that really took advantage of functionality, simplicity, and enjoyment of browsing the page. I feel it's very fun to navigate, and I hope you agree.
